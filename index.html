HI! I'M PETER ZAFFINA

I help enterprise leadership teams modernize technology and operating models so they can scale securely and reliably, without the disruption, vendor dependency, or transformation fatigue that usually comes with large-scale change.

How I Engage


My work focuses on executive decision-making, operating model clarity, and governance readiness. I do not operate as an implementer or program manager. I partner with senior leaders and boards at moments where judgment, alignment, and risk management matter most.

Enterprise Technology Operating Model Review

  • A focused review of how technology actually operates across the enterprise, assessed against business strategy, risk posture, and growth objectives.
  • This engagement helps executive teams and boards understand whether their current structure, decision rights, and incentives are enabling progress or quietly increasing exposure.
  • The outcome is a concise executive brief that clarifies structural risks, sources of leverage, and leadership-level actions required to strengthen reliability and accountability.

Executive Decision Support for Large-Scale Change

  • Strategic advisory support for senior leaders navigating high-stakes technology decisions where the cost of misalignment exceeds the cost of delay.
  • I help executives pressure-test assumptions, surface second-order impacts, and evaluate tradeoffs across platform strategy, sourcing models, and organizational change.
  • This work is designed to strengthen decision quality and executive confidence during periods of transformation, without introducing additional delivery complexity.

Board-Level Technology and Risk Readiness

  • Support for executive teams preparing to engage boards on technology posture, investment decisions, and enterprise risk.
  • I help translate complex technical realities into clear governance narratives, enabling boards to ask the right questions and executives to communicate with clarity and credibility.
  • The focus is not compliance or reporting, but shared understanding and durable oversight that enables sustained progress.
